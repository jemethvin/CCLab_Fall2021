Gigi D'Agostino (Luigino Celestino di Agostino) is an Italian DJ, singer, and record producer.
He began working as a DJ in 1986, but his largest chart successes all came in the years 1999 and 2000. Patience is a virtue!
His most popular releases include L'Amor Toujours, Bla Bla Bla, Another Way, and The Riddle. I think all these songs are great, and suggest you go listen to them. Thank me later!
